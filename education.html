Education and Skilling Wing

Building on our 15+ years of experience in rural healthcare, we've expanded our mission to create self-sustaining communities through comprehensive education and skill development programs. Our approach combines practical training with community empowerment to create lasting change.

Our Focus

Comprehensive programs designed for community development.

Skill Development

Training volunteers and students in essential medical support skills including patient care assistance, basic health screening procedures, and camp organization to support our medical outreach programs.

Women Empowerment

Empowering women nurses and lab technicians with advanced training in maternal health, child care, and community health education to lead healthcare initiatives in remote areas.

Youth Development

Developing young nursing students and volunteers through hands-on experience in medical camps, teaching them practical healthcare delivery skills and building confidence for rural healthcare service.

Education & Skilling Program

Three comprehensive programs designed to create lasting change through education, skills development, and community empowerment.

Healthcare Training

Training village health workers, nurses, and community health champions to build local healthcare capacity.

  • Basic medical training
  • Community health education
  • Maternal & child health care
