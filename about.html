About Okti Foundation

Empowering women through healthcare access, women's empowerment, and sustainable health since 2009.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide equitable healthcare access to underserved communities, with special focus on women's health, cancer prevention, and community empowerment across rural India.

Our Vision

To create a healthier, more empowered India where every individual, regardless of their location or economic status, has access to quality healthcare and health education.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and understanding, treating each person with dignity and respect.

Equity

We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental right that should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

Service

We are dedicated to serving our communities with excellence, integrity, and a commitment to making a lasting impact.

Our Story

The Beginning - 2009

Founded with a vision to bridge the healthcare gap in rural India, Okti Foundation began as a small initiative to provide medical services to underserved communities.

Our Model

We developed a unique mobile healthcare model that brings specialized medical services directly to rural areas, eliminating barriers of distance and accessibility.

Community Participation

We believe in empowering communities to take charge of their health through education, training, and sustainable healthcare programs.
